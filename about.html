ABOUT HERBALIFE

Introduction

Herbalife is the world's no1 Nutrition company that was founded out of tragedy in the United States of America by Mark Hughes in 1980 at the age of 24. Mark Hughes's dream of founding Herbalife was triggered by the death of his mother who died as a result of unhealthy weight management as she was a modeler. He founded Herbalife to provide a solution of a healthy weight management as well as overall health and wellness.

Today, we are the global health and wellness community dedicated to helping you live your best life. For over 40 years and in more than 90 countries, we have empowered millions of people to make real changes their lives through our science-backed products, the guidance of a coach - what we call a Herbalife Independent Distributor - and the opportunity to build a business, and we are just getting started!

At Herbalife, we believe that the best lives are balanced lives. Through our Independent Distributors, who foster community, personal growth, and business opportunities, we provide a wide range of products designed to help you achieve your health and wellness goals.

You bring the determination to start, and we will support you every step of the way - helping you improve your nutrition, wellness, and overall lifestyle while offeing a path to financial growth through your Herbalife business.

Our Vision

We are committed to becoming the most trusted nutrition and lifestyle company in the world. Our vision aligns with the company and drives our growth: Our values guide our behavior and inform our actions. We always do what is right.

Our Mission

To improve people's lives by providing access to personalised nutrition and supportive community, so everyone can pursue a healthy, active life. Our focus is to empower individuals globally to achieve a healthy, active lifestyle.
