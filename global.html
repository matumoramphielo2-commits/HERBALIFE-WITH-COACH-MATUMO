GLOBAL OPERATIONS

Herbalife caters to a variety of daily nutritional needs, offering choices to consumers in over 90 countries across the globe(56 countries in EMEA)

Regions we serve

  • Asia Pacific: The largest market, accounting for roughly 33.86% of net sales as of 2023
  • North America: Representing 22.35% of sales as of 2023
  • EMEA(Europe,Middle East, and Africa): Covering over 56 countries in these regions contributing 21.11% of sales in 2023
  • Latin America: Accounting for 162% of sales in 2023
  • China: Representing 6.47% of sales as of 2023

Operational Hubs

Herbalife operates a decentralised global operations model, with key hubs focusing on manufactoring, distribution, and business service rather than a single central location

    Key global sites:
  • Corporate Headquaters: Los Angles, California, U.S.A
  • Major Distribution Hub: Venray, Netherlands(serves international markets)
  • Innovation and Manufacturing Facility: Winston-Salem, North California, U.S.A(Large-scale manufacturing)
  • APAC Shared Services Center: Kuala Lumpur, Malaysia(handles finance, accounting, and ITfor the Asia Pacific region
